# *CITIZENSHIP*

## Article 5 THE REPUBLIC OF UGANDA
(1) Uganda is one Sovereign State and a Republic.

(2) Uganda shall consist of the districts specified in the First Schedule to this Constitution and
such other districts as may be established in accordance with this Constitution or any other law
consistent with it.

(3) The territorial boundary of Uganda shall be as delineated in the Second Schedule to this
Constitution.

(4) The capital of Uganda is Kampala.

## Article 6 Official Language
( 1) The official language of Uganda is English.

(2) Subject to clause (1) of this article, any other language may be used as a medium of
instruction in schools or other educational institutions or for legislative, administrative or judicial
purposes as may be prescribed by law.

## Article 7 Non-adoption of State religion
Uganda shall not adopt a State religion.

## Article 8 National symbols and seals
The National Flag, the National Coat of Arms, the Public Seal, the National Anthem and the seals
of the Courts of Judicature in use immediately before the coming into force of this Constitution,
shall continue to be in use.
